01 How to join

  1. Install Prism Launcher

    Grab Prism Launcher (free, open-source, Windows/Mac/Linux). You'll need a Minecraft account and Java 21, which Prism offers to download for you if it's missing.

  2. Download the pack

    Grab the Derpack X pack file (~5 MB). One small zip, no account or store needed.

  3. Import into Prism

    Add Instance → Import from zip → Browse to the file → OK. It unpacks small, with no mod jars yet; those come on first launch.

  4. Launch once to download mods

    Hit Launch. The first run pauses a minute or two while it fetches every mod (the Prism log shows progress). Later launches are instant. The instance ships pre-tuned at 8 GB min / 12 GB max. Lower the max under Edit → Settings → Java if you have less than 12 GB, but never below 4 GB.

  5. Connect

    Multiplayer → Add Server, then paste the address:

    mc.ishimura.xyz

Five systems, one world

It's broad on purpose — food, magic, colonies, mobs, vehicles — but it isn't a random heap. Everything hangs off five big systems, and because ores are scarce and regional, no one can comfortably run all five alone. You pick what you're good at, and trade for the rest.

Create — the tech spine

The rotational-engineering core everything else bolts onto: Big Cannons, Create Nuclear, TaCZ firearms gated behind Create parts, and dozens of add-ons for factories, logistics and jetpacks.

Aeronautics & logistics

The flagship. Create Aeronautics physics-simulated airships and planes, trains via Steam 'n' Rails, and a compat layer that keeps third-party mods behaving on moving contraptions.

Magic

Three magic mods bridged into one progression: Ars Nouveau, Iron's Spells 'n Spellbooks and Occultism, with Modular Golems and Grimoire of Gaia alongside.

Economy & trade

The reason to specialize. Numismatics currency, Create: Trading Floor and bounties give your surplus a buyer, while MineColonies towns and TownTalk villagers fill the world out.

Survival & seasons

The world pushing back. Serene Seasons, Cold Sweat temperature and Spice of Life variety mean what you farm and where you settle actually matter through the year.

…and a fuller, deadlier world

Hanging off those five: Alex's and Mowzie's Mobs, Cataclysm and Bosses of Mass Destruction (whose drops unlock late-game tech), Farmer's Delight and the Let's Do series, plus Terralith, YUNG's dungeons and stacks of building blocks.

Complex Create tech unlocks through colony progress and boss drops, not a tech-tree grind. It's pre-alpha and stays fresh through curated updates rather than world wipes, so the installer always pulls the current set and auto-syncs each launch.

    05 FAQ

    It won't launch or it crashes on world load.

    Most of the time it's too little RAM or a half-finished first download. Make sure Java 21 is installed, give the instance 8 GB, and let the first launch finish fetching mods. Still stuck? Open .minecraft/logs/latest.log, find the Caused by line, and drop the log in the Discord.

    My airship looks broken with shaders on.

    A known upstream bug with Iris/Oculus shaders: they render Aeronautics ships wrong. Turn shaders off when flying.

    A mod misbehaves while I'm on a moving ship.

    The compat layer is meant to handle exactly that. If a specific mod is still wrong on a contraption, mention which one in the Discord so it can be flagged.

    Can I add my own mods?

    Client-only cosmetic mods are usually fine. Anything touching world content or networking will desync you from the server, so ask first.

    How do I report a bug or request a mod?

    Post in the Discord. That's the fastest way to reach the people who run the pack, and you don't need an account anywhere else.
