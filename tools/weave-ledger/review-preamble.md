## These reviews are the thunderdome

The roadmap runs a curation "thunderdome" (merit review) on each odd version. This doc operationalizes
it: per milestone, that pillar's mods come up with their full weave evidence, and the human verdict is
recorded by ticking the mod in its `docs/MOD-REVIEW.md` issue (the schedule) ‚Äî keep, cut, or author the
listed weaves. The Gate-2 sign-off on the weave candidates and the thunderdome keep/cut call are the
**same decision**, made once, per mod, in that mod's milestone.

## How to read an entry

For each mod: `modid (packwiz-slug) ‚Äî NA/NR ¬∑ motifs` (accept/reject counts + the motifs its ACCEPTs
ride), then a **DX:** line ‚Äî my own take (agree/disagree, risk, priority, cross-links, cut/Gate-0
flags) ‚Äî then the top consensus-**ACCEPT** weaves, each as `motif √óN ‚Üí pillar ¬∑ from ¬∑ via method`,
where `√óN` = how many independent passes proposed it (confidence). Motif definitions live in
`docs/WEAVE-LEDGER.md` ¬ß1 **on the `claude/weaving-plan` branch**.

## The signal: consensus, not volume

Of the full corpus, the actionable set is the high-`times` ACCEPTs ‚Äî a weave proposed once is noise; one
proposed by 20-30 passes (`dynamictreesplus` saguaro-milling, `deeperdarker` soul‚Üíspirit_fire,
`tfmg` diesel‚Üíengine, `cold_sweat` hearth-gate) is a near-certain author. Spend review attention there.

## Authoring rides a different milestone than review

A weave is **reviewed** in its mod's milestone but often **authored** in the pillar it reaches *into* ‚Äî
a Create mod whose best weave is an aeronautics hull part is reviewed in v0.7.0, authored in v0.9.0. The
DX notes call this out per mod.
