  registered recipe-types (AUTO, jar-grounded): dynamictrees:mega_seed, dynamictrees:seed_conversion
  non-recipe mechanics (SWEEP): algorithmic tree growth (cellular-automata leaves + branch logic, per-species), trees mature/wither with light & rainfall, whole-tree felling drops all logs at once, Fortune on axes boosts log yield, self-seeding on suitable ground. The recipe types just convert vanilla saplings↔dynamic seeds (incl. mega variants), using a dirt bucket.
  consumes / outputs (SWEEP): consumes vanilla/modded saplings + bucket-of-dirt to mint species seeds; outputs ordinary logs/sticks/saplings on harvest (vanilla item economy). No foreign-material method beyond sapling conversion.
