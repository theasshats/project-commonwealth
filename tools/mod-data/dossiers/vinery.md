  non-recipe mechanics (SWEEP): grapevine cultivation (stems/slabs/lattices grow grape variants by climate; jungle grapes on lattices); grapevine_pot stomps grapes→grape juice (jump in, extract with bottles); fermentation_barrel takes grape juice + 4 ingredient slots + wine bottle → named wine (~12s/batch); apple_press: apples→apple_mash. Bottled wines age in-world (1 "year" per ~20 days) for stronger drink effects. Winemaker villager trades (baskets/wines).
  consumes / outputs (SWEEP): CONSUMES grapes, apples, cherries, generic fruit/sugar in ingredient slots, glass bottles; OUTPUTS grape_juice, apple_juice, apple_cider, applesauce, apple_mash, and dozens of effect-bearing wines — all high-value finished consumables (luxury trade goods).
