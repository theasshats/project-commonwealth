one-line:   Adds Touhou-themed "little maid" companion entities that work 21 task modes (combat/danmaku, farming, fishing, ranching, item pickup) plus an Altar multiblock that summons maids and crafts items by spending power points.
vibe:       Japanese anime / Touhou fandom — whimsical, cute companions; shrine-maiden (miko) aesthetic
signature:  the maids themselves, the Altar multiblock, Hakurei's Gohei (danmaku staff / altar activator), the Broom (rideable — flight when a maid sits on it), Aya's Camera (photo→re-summon a captured maid), maid backpacks/beacon

methods (FIRST-CLASS — what this mod can DO; the resources other mods pull THROUGH — §2.0):
  registered recipe-types (AUTO, jar-grounded): touhou_little_maid:altar_recipe_serializers
  non-recipe mechanics (SWEEP): Altar multiblock (Torii + pillars, activated by right-clicking with a Gohei) — places item inputs on towers and, given enough power points, yields an output at center; the mod's whole progression is altar-gated (no crafting table for its content). Power points come from throwing power-point bottles (village loot / maid-fairy drops), ~0.5 each like an XP bottle. Maids are automation entities: they plant/harvest crops, fish, shear, milk, breed, fight; have affection/inventory/GUI. Photo/camera capture system. Heavily KubeJS/CraftTweaker-extensible recipes (dep: kubejs).
  consumes / outputs (SWEEP): CONSUMES — power-point bottles (its currency) + vanilla mats in default altar recipes (a maid = lapis + diamond + gold + redstone + iron + coal; broom = hay + sticks + ender eye; camera = quartz + obsidian). OUTPUTS — maids, brooms, cameras, decorative/utility items. Altar recipe-type can ACCEPT arbitrary modded item inputs and gate on power points (KubeJS-addable).
