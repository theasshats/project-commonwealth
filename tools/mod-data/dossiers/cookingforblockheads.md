methods (FIRST-CLASS — what this mod can DO; the resources other mods pull THROUGH — §2.0):
  registered recipe-types (AUTO, jar-grounded): cookingforblockheads:toaster
  non-recipe mechanics (SWEEP): the Cooking Table is the hub of a connected kitchen multiblock — it scans linked counters/fridges and surfaces only the food recipes you can currently craft (Book II auto-crafts them); the Sink supplies infinite water to recipes; the Oven cooks food faster; the Toaster (its one registered recipe-type) toasts bread→toast; the Cow Jar/Milk Jar provide renewable milk. It is primarily a *recipe-discovery & batch-crafting convenience layer over other mods' food recipes*, not a new processing chain. [src CF/wiki]
  consumes / outputs (SWEEP): consumes whatever food ingredients are stocked in connected storage and crafts vanilla/modded food recipes; Toaster consumes bread. Join key: it aggregates and surfaces the OUTPUT of every food mod (Farmer's Delight, Vinery, Meadow…), so it is naturally a downstream sink for the survival/food cluster rather than a source.
