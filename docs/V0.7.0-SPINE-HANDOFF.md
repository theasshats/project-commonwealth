## 4. Integration designs (chat-only until now)

### 4a. `create-low-heated` — heat ladder (load-bearing)
- The mod **disables Blaze Burner passive heating** and adds a **"low heat"** temperate tier via `basic_burner`.
  Low heat = the *old passive heat* for steam engines.
- New heat ladder: **low heat (`basic_burner`) → heated (Blaze Burner) → superheated (Blaze Burner + Blaze Cake)**.
- This makes the **steam gate (rung 3)** a real dependency: you must build a `basic_burner` for low heat to run
  a basic boiler — strengthens "steam is earned" (no free passive heat).
- Work: use low heat as a recipe heat-level for temperate T2 processing; reserve heated/superheated for T3+;
  **update `CREATE-SPINE.md` Part 1 (rung 3)** + `20-power-ladder.js` to document the heat ladder as the steam gate.

### 4b. `createoreexcavation` — T4 end-game resource SINK
- Reframe (not a cut): gate the drilling machine + drill tiers (brass→diamond→netherite) behind **T4**
  (TFMG circuits / electric / boss-tier materials); route drill heads **through Create** (pressing/deploying);
  make drill heads **consumable** + tie throughput to **high power draw**. T4-locked so it can't bust early/mid
  scarcity — at end-game it *spends* surplus power/effort for ore (a sink, not an early faucet). ~3–6 recipes.
